Become an Insider

Open to all SRM students from any year or department. The selection process is transparent and merit-based.

1

Application

Fill out the interest form sharing your skills and domain preferences.

2

Task Round

Complete a domain-specific task demonstrating your abilities.

3

Short Interview

A brief conversation about your goals and alignment with our vision.

4

Background Check

Final verification before you officially join the community.
